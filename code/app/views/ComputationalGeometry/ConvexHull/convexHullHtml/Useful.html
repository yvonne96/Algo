When is Convex Hull useful?

One application of convex hull that is used in real life is to "relax" a non-convex problem. So in other words we are trying to find the closest representation of a convex problem to a non complex problem which your are trying to solve. This is handy as if you had many points with loads of minima and then construct a relaxation of these points using local and global minima, your convex surface will contain the true global maxima.
