Step by Step Example

let us take the example "4627" as our example

First Iteration

       lst
       
    (4 _ 6 2 7)     6 is the first element
                      to be positioned
       
    (4 6 _ 2 7)     since 4 is less than 6,
                      6 is positioned in
                       the blank spot

Second Iteration

      
    (4 6 _ 2 7)     2 is the next element
                      to be positioned
       
    (4 _ 6 2 7)     since 6 is not less than 2
                     the blank moves left
       
    (_ 4 6 2 7)     since 4 is not less than 2
                     the blank moves left again
       
    (2 4 6 _ 7)     since we are at the end of
                     the list 2 is placed in
                        the blanks spot

Third Iteration

      
    (2 4 6 _ 7)    7 is the next element
                      to be positioned
       
    (2 4 6 7 _)    since 6 is less than 7,
                      7 is positioned in
                       the blank spot
