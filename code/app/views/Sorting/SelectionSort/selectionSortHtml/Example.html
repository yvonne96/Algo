Step by Step Example

lets take the array "1 4 2 3" as our example.

Purple : num is array[i] and min_found

Red : min_found

Blue : array[i]

Orange : array[j]

First Iteration

(1 4 2 3)
   ...
 (1 4 2 3)  since 1 is the smallest it doesn't change

Second Iteration

(1 4 2 3)

 (1 4 2 3)

 (1 2 4 3) 2 is the smallest so it swaps with 4

Third Iteration

(1 2 4 3)

 (1 2 4 3)

 (1 2 3 4) 3 is the smallest so it swaps with 4

So in the end, the sorted array is "1 2 3 4"
